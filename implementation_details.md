# Cozy Pastel Cat Avatar Upgrades

This update shifts the Cat Pet avatar from a cool-toned periwinkle scene to a warm, sunlit pastel afternoon/sunset cozy room experience.

## 🎨 New Warm Pastel Palette
We expanded the 8-bit palette (up to 256 colors supported by `M5Canvas`) to use warm and cozy tones:
* **Background Wall**: Cozy warm pastel peach.
* **Floorboards**: Warm honey-beige wood floor with skirting boards and seam lines.
* **Lines**: Warm cocoa/soft brown-grey line color for details.
* **Tabby Cat**: Warm cream body, golden/ginger stripes, strawberry-pink ears/cheeks/nose, and clean white chest/socks.
* **Eyes**: Slate black pupils with a warm amber/golden iris highlight.
* **Paws / Socks**: Cute white paws on all four legs.
* **Tail**: Alternating cream and ginger-peach stripes with a white tip, pink center, and a cute pink ribbon bow.
* **Plate & Food**: Soft sage-green bowl with tasty orange kibbles.
* **Rug**: Cozy cream-yellow rug with a soft coral-pink pattern and fringes.
* **Window**: Warm beige wooden pane frame, soft sunset pink background sky, warm olive-green hills, and a glowing warm sunset sun.
* **Plant**: Warm terracotta pot with soft sage-green stems and leaves.
* **Garland**: A multi-colored string of warm pastel flags (apricot, coral, yellow, and cream).
* **Zzz / Particles**: Cozy warm golden-yellow Zzz symbols.

---

## 🍃 Rich Interactive Environment
The room is now a lively, layered environment:
1. **Garland**: Draped across the top of the wall, with flags that sway in the breeze.
2. **Cozy Window**: Shows a glowing sunset sun, rolling green hills, and a fluffy white cloud that slowly drifts across the pane.
3. **Animated Rug**: Centered on the floor with patterned textures and side fringes where the cat walks.
4. **Waving Potted Plant**: Stems and leaf clusters that gently sway on the left side of the room.
5. **Interactive Food Plate**: Kibbles disappear one by one as the cat eats, and then magical refills happen when the cat heads to the plate again.

---

## 🏃 Expression Particles & Animations
We added animations and expressions that run deterministically:
* **Walking Bob**: Instead of sliding, the cat now bobbles up and down while walking, creating a cute waddling effect.
* **Breathing Idle**: When sitting, eating, or playing, the cat's body gently rises and falls (idle breathing) to make it feel alive.
* **Cuteness Tail**: A tail with alternating cream and ginger stripes, a white tip, and a cute pink ribbon bow tied around it that wiggles as the tail swings!
* **Mood Particles**:
  * **Happy (Hearts)**: Floating pastel red/pink hearts rise and drift from the head.
  * **Sleepy (Zzz)**: Rising and fading warm golden "Z" letters that scale and float upward.
  * **Angry (Anger Vein)**: A twitching red anger vein cross pops up near the head.
  * **Sad (Sweat/Tears)**: Soft blue sweat drops slide down the side of the head.
* **Low Battery Sadness**: If the battery drops below 20%, the cat will default to a **Sad** mood with sliding tears, encouraging you to plug in the charger!
* **Ground Shadow**: A soft shadow under the cat stretches/shrinks when jumping or moving.

---

## 🔋 Stylized Battery Pod
* Replaced the standard battery indicator with a rounded capsule styled to fit the pastel theme.
* Color shifts based on level: mint green (>60%), pastel orange (25-60%), or rose pink (<25%).
* Displays a pulsing white lightning bolt in the center when charging.
